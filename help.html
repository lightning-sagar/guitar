How to Use the Virtual Guitar

Welcome to the Virtual Guitar!

Explore and enjoy the Virtual Guitar with two easy methods:

Method 1: Mouse Over

Move your mouse pointer over the keys to make beautiful guitar sounds play.

Method 2: Key Down

Play the guitar with your keyboard. Just press the keys on your keyboard, and the guitar sounds will follow your tune.

Note: Make sure the "Off KeyDown" button is not clicked to enable this feature.
